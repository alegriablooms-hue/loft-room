101" × 133" × 101.5"

A compact room (8'5" × 11'1", 8'5½" ceiling) doing triple duty: a lofted sleeping platform, a dedicated sewing station under the window, and an office desk tucked beneath the loft.

Good news: the ceiling now works for a real loft

At 101.5" (8'5½"), a standing loft is viable — IKEA VITVAL (needs 94½") fits, and the Wayfair Harriet Bee and Target loft beds (~65–69" tall, ~54" to the loft base) leave full headroom below for the desk and plenty above. Only IKEA's tall STORÅ (needs 106") still misses, by ~4½".

How the three zones fit

Right wall: loft bed up top, office desk + chair underneath, ladder at the foot.

Left wall (window): sewing bench for daylight, pegboard above, drawers below.

Center-left: cube storage + rolling cart, rug anchoring the open floor.

Watch-outs

• Keep the 60" window clear of tall storage so daylight reaches the sewing bench.

• Leave swing clearance for the 28" and 29" doors on the right/bottom walls.

• Confirm the chosen loft's footprint (~42" × 80" twin) against the 101" wall before buying.
