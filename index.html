How the pieces fit

Left wall: the tall white KALLAX shelf for bins, books and plants.

Center: the white NORDEN gateleg table on the rug — opened up for sewing/crafts, folded flat to free the floor.

Right & bottom walls: white BRYTNING desk + dark-green IDÅSEN cabinet, and the white HEMNES dresser by the entry.

Watch-outs

• The Twin XL loft is 92½" long — confirm that run plus the ladder clears the back wall and the top-right closet before buying.

• The IDÅSEN cabinet is the one dark accent; everything else is white + pecan wood, so keep extra textiles neutral to let it pop.

• Leave swing clearance for the entry and the right-wall door; the NORDEN table's drop leaves let you reclaim the walkway when folded.
